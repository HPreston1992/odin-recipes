Stuffed Peppers

Beef, tomato sauce, and cheese stuffed into peppers

Ingredients

  • 8 green peppers
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 64 oz of tomato sauce
  • 2 pieces of sliced Velveeta Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cut the top of the peppers off
  3. Boil the peppers in water for 6-8 minutes
  4. Brown ground beef
  5. Once meat is brown add in tomato sauce and rice
  6. After the meat sauce and rice has thickened add the cheese and cover
  7. Once cheese has softened mix into mixture
  8. Fill peppers with meat and rice
  9. Bake for 1 hour
